SBD – A Local Band from Sierra Vista, Arizona (1998-2001)

SBD was a local band from Sierra Vista, Arizona, active from 1998 to 2001. They were part of the underground music scene and performed at local venues, including the Sierra Vista Bandshell. Their most documented performance was at the April 1, 2000, Battle of the Bands, where they played a cover of “My Own Summer (Shove It)” by Deftones. This performance was recorded and later broadcast on KBHS TV 3 on April 7, 2000.
